Who is God to us?

The Holy communion is a secret time of felowship with the Lord, where believers remember or remind themselves of the supreme sacrifice of Jesus on the cross of calvary (1st Peter 1:18 & 19). The Holy communion is also known as the Lord's supper, Holy Eucharist or Holy sacrament. Holy communion is the center of the church life, (Mark 6:54), Everything we do in the church leads to the Eucharist and all things flow from it. It is the completion of all the church sacrament and the source, gold of the church doctrine and institution.
