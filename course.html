It is a hidden truth. The communion is not exceptional, there is a mystery behind the Holy communion which the church should know and explore. The early church discovered and enjoyed it, they did not take the Lord's supper lightly. They were so particular about it as they broke bread daily both in the temple and in house to house (Acts 2:46). Holy communion is one vital tool we should be armed with and put to work or celebrate if we are to enjoy the many sided blessing Jesus paid for at calvary and made maximum impact in life of a christian.
